About Timedrift

Solo creators are the future of games — but the industry isn't built for them yet.

Timedrift is a collective designed for AI-native game makers:

  • We hire talented solo developers full-time.
  • We provide structure, salary, and milestone support.
  • You keep ownership of your game.
  • We launch and scale together — sharing in the upside.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects does Timedrift support?

Solo-led, original game projects with clear creative vision and strong execution potential.

Do creators keep ownership of their games?

Yes. Creators retain full ownership. We back you — we don't own you.

What does backing include?

Salary, benefits, milestone structure, publishing support, launch help, and creative freedom.

What happens if a project doesn’t work out?

No penalties. We pivot or start fresh — together. Shots on goal are part of the strategy.

Is this a freelance gig or a full-time job?

Full-time employment with salary and benefits during the residency.

How selective is Timedrift?

Selective — but human. If you’re serious about your craft, we want to hear from you.
