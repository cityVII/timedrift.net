Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of projects does Timedrift back?

Original, solo-led games with a clear creative spark — and the chops to pull it off.

Do creators keep control of their games?

Yes. It’s your vision. We back you — we don’t own you.

What does backing include?

Salary, benefits, milestone support, publishing help, launch help — and real creative freedom.

What if a project doesn’t work out?

No penalties. We pivot, restart, or build the next shot — together.

Is this freelance or full-time?

Full-time employment — with salary, benefits, and space to build your best work.

How selective is Timedrift?

Selective — but human. If you’re serious about your craft, we want to hear your story.
